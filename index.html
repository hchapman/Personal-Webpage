    About Me

    I am currently a mathematics graduate student at the University of Georgia working towards a Ph.D. degree. During the 2012-2013 school year, I was supported by the University of Georgia's VIGRE II grant. I graduated from Bowdoin College in May of 2011 with a B.A. in Mathematics and Computer Science, with Honors in Mathematics.
